Homeland Moon, Canada Love 2020 Mid-Autumn Festival Celebration

On September 26, 2020 (Saturday) Brampton Association of Chinese Performing Arts is going to host an online gala to celebrate the Moon Festival, the theme is "Homeland Moon, Canada Love" via Zoom.

2020, year of the Gengzi, is destined to be unusual. Within the first half of the year, we experienced various disasters: Covid-19 over the world, locust plague in Eastern Africa, snowstorm in Spain, bush fires in Australia, forest fire in Amazon, volcano eruption in Philippines, etc.

Therefore some say the goal of 2020 is simply to survive. However we aim at living a wonderful and beautiful life rather than just surviving. As Mid Autumn Festival (October 01, 2020) is approaching, we cordially invite you to join us in the celebration gala via Zoom at 10 AM on September 26, 2020.

The celebration gala will be posted on Facebook, YouTube, WeChat, TangDou Dancing App, website of Brampton City, website of our organization, and other media. More than 300 online audience are anticipated. We warmly welcome sponsorship from business and individuals. Sponsor of $500 and above will be linked to the website of our organization for advertisement. All sponsors will be displayed in the sponsorship list. Here is our website: BAOCPA.org and here is our email: admin@baocpa.org

Come and join us, let's dance with music, let's sing with music. Enrich your life, enhance your immune system, beat the virus, be healthy and happy. Looking forward to your participation and support!
