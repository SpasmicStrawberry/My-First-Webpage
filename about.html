About Us

We, Brampton Association of Chinese Performing Arts was founded in May 2019. Our current members come from more than ten countries and regions. Our mission is “To become happy and healthy through dancing”. In the past year, we learned more than 30 dances and songs, and we were invited to perform in a few multi-culturual events in Toronto Nathan Philip Square, and GTA area. We successfully launched an art performance in Kennedy Square in December 2019, which attracted hundreds of local residents. The president of our organization is Jennifer Lemon, the vice president is Julia Li. There is a five people committee board. The president, vice president and committee members are well-educated professionals from all fields with outstanding leadership and organization skills. Currently we have a dancing team and a singing team with a total of 56 registered members. Team of TaiJi and team of model show are in process.
